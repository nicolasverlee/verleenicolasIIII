Nicolas Verlee



Born in Ghent, this artist creates a different reality by using vivid colors and different mediums. Nowadays his artwork represents voyeuristic images. Also inspired by paranoiac thoughts, the influence of todays social media and the effect of surveillance camera's in our society.
